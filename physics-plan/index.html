Where to Focus

Priority Matrix

Ranked by combined importance × difficulty. Do not skip Tier 1 topics.

⚡ Tier 1 — Highest Stakes
01 Riemann curvature tensor & Bianchi identities
02 Covariant derivative & parallel transport
03 Einstein Field Equations from Hilbert action
04 Dirac equation: Lorentz covariance, spinors, CPT
05 Fock space & second quantization
06 Path integral in QFT
07 QFT on curved spacetime — Hawking & Unruh
08 Clebsch-Gordan decomposition
09 Spectral theorem (infinite-dimensional)
10 Lie algebra representations (roots & weights)
★ Tier 2 — High Priority, Build Early
01 Bra-ket formalism, observables, measurement postulate
02 Angular momentum algebra — SO(3), SU(2)
03 Lagrangian & Hamiltonian mechanics
04 Residue theorem & contour integration
05 Differential forms & exterior derivative
06 Maxwell equations in covariant form
07 Fourier analysis (transforms + series)
08 Noether's theorem (mechanics & fields)
◦ Tier 3 — Revise Quickly
01 Special functions (Legendre, Bessel, Hermite)
02 Laplace & Fourier transforms
03 First & second order ODEs
04 Basic group axioms & cosets
05 SR kinematics & 4-vectors
06 Harmonic oscillator (QM)
